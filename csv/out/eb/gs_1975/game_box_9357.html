Firebirds Clip Express 7-6 in GCS Contest
The Phoenix Firebirds knotted up the Grand Championship Series at Express Stadium today, thanks in large part to first baseman Nick Perez. Perez was 3-4 with 2 doubles and a walk, while adding 3 RBI and scoring once as he overpowered the Pittsburgh Express, enabling Phoenix to grab the 7-6 win and move into a 1-1 tie in the best-of-7 series.

Had Perez not come through in the top of the eighth, the outcome might have changed. Instead, with two down, Perez hit a bases-clearing double. The Firebirds never looked back after taking the 7-6 lead.

"We'll put that win in the can," said Perez, "and go after the next one."

The next game is set to be played for Tuesday, October 28th , 1975 at Phoenix Grounds in Phoenix.
