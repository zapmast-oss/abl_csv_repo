Game 5 was big. So was Arthur Blin.

In leading Phoenix to a crucial 3-2 victory over the Pittsburgh Express at Phoenix Grounds today, the Phoenix starter rose to the occasion, tossing 6 good innings. Blin threw 6.0 innings of 5-hit ball as the Firebirds defended their home field to seize a 3-2 lead in the best-of-7 Grand Championship Series.

Jeff Shire was the walk-off hero for Phoenix. With the game tied in the bottom of the ninth inning, the Firebirds center fielder hit a dramatic run-scoring double to send the teams home.

In postgame remarks, Shire said: "To all who've been doubting my effort -- I hope they saw this game."
