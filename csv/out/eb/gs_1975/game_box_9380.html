Phoenix Wins it All
It was a hard-fought battle for both teams to make it this far. But as the saying goes, "to the victors go the spoils." For the victorious Phoenix Firebirds, that meant the Action Baseball League Grand Championship Series trophy.

The Firebirds took a 3-1 victory over the Pittsburgh Express at Express Stadium, winning their 1st championship after taking the series 4-3.

"We've got grit," said Firebirds first baseman Nick Perez, who was named MVP of the series. "We know how to win when we have to."

The new titleholders completed the regular season with a second-place finish in the National Baseball Conference Western Division and a 89-73 record.
