Dallas Rustlers Win Grand Championship Series
The Dallas Rustlers were good enough to get past the Houston Mavericks at Rustlers Grounds today and secure the 1st championship in franchise history.

"When you wear Rustlers on your shirt, you're good at something," Dallas skipper Glenn Brophy told the press.

Rustlers right fielder Gary McMinn was named MVP of the series after he went .421 with 2 home runs and 5 runs batted in.

"It feels totally incredible," McMinn said. "I could live to 1,000 years old and I'd remember this moment."

The new champions capped off a great season with a 1-0 victory over the Mavericks to win the Grand Championship Series 4-3.
