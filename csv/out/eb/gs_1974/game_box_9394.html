Pivotal Game 5 Goes to Host Express, 2-0
The bats were silent for much of the day at Express Stadium today. But Pittsburgh designated hitter Antonio Serrano conjured up just enough offense to carry the Express past the Miami Hurricanes, 2-0. In a game dominated by the pitching staffs, Serrano was 3-4 with a double and 2 singles. The Express now lead 3-2 in the best-of-7 series.

Making an impact for the home team was Tony Sotelo. In the bottom of the fifth, the 29-year old first baseman had a run-scoring walk. That made the score 2-0, Express.

"I just tried to see the ball and put a good swing on it," said Serrano.

Game 6 is scheduled for Monday at Hurricanes Park in Miami.
