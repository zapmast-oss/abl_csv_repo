Mavericks Even With Firebirds After Walk-Off Win
The Houston Mavericks nearly wasted a strong game from starter Kyle Chambers, but were saved by some timely hitting in a walk-off win over the Phoenix Firebirds today.

Chambers threw 7.0 innings of 5-hit ball. The win allowed Houston to force a 1-1 tie in the best-of-7 Grand Championship Series.

The clutch at-bat came in the bottom of the tenth inning, when Houston center fielder Apostolos Georghiou, facing Jacob Sutherland, had a run-scoring single. It gave the Mavericks the 6-5 win.

"It wasn't our best performance," said Chambers, "but a win in the playoffs is nothing to sniff at."

The next game is set to be played for Friday, October 26th , 1979 at Phoenix Grounds in Phoenix.
