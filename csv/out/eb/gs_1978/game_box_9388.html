Miami Wins at Home to Narrow Series Deficit to 3-2
Bobby Paez was not about to watch an opponent celebrate a championship on his home field.

The right-hander pitched the Miami Hurricanes to victory at Hurricanes Park today, staving off the Houston Mavericks, who remain one game away from a title. Paez threw 8.1 innings of 5-hit ball. The win cuts the series deficit for the Hurricanes to 3-2 in the best-of-7 series.

Had Cory Lynn not delivered a key base hit in the bottom of the eighth, the outcome might have been different. Instead, with two down and a runner on 3rd, Lynn hit a run-scoring single. That made the score 4-3, in favor of the Hurricanes.

"Winning the game is always the first objective," Paez said.

The next game is set to be played for Monday, October 30th , 1978 at Houston Stadium in Houston.
