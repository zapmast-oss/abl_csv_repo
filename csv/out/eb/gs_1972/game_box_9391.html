The Atlanta Kings pulled out all the stops tonight, winning the Grand Championship Series in a hard-fought series with the Denver Rocketeers.

It was a seesaw affair, but with a 9-8 victory at Kings Field, Atlanta lay claim to the franchise's 1st championship.

"There's no denying we faced a tough opponent," said Jose Martinez. "But as the old saying goes, 'when the going gets tough, the tough get going,' and that's what we did today."

The champs finished the regular season 97-65 and claimed second place in the National Baseball Conference Eastern Division.

A victory parade is set to be schedule in Atlanta in the days to come.
