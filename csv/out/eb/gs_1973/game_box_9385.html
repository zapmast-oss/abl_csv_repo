Sawatsky Blasts Fire, Rocketeers Take Game 3, 10-5
Riding the 2-hit performance of left fielder Robbie Sawatsky, the Denver Rocketeers stopped the visiting Chicago Fire, 10-5, in Game 3 of the Grand Championship Series today. Sawatsky went 2-4 with a home run and a walk, while scoring 2 times and driving in 3 for the Rocketeers, who now lead the best-of-7 series, 2 games to 1.

Jayson Harrison hit a 2-run triple off Nelson Majano in the bottom of the sixth, which put the Rocketeers in front, 6-4. Harrison had 1 walk, in addition to the triple. For the game, he went 1 for 3.

Harrison called the triumph "a good team win."

The next game is set to be played tomorrow at Rocketeers Field in Denver.
