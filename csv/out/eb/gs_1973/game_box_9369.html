Fire Hammered by Pena, Drop Game 1 to Rocketeers
With second baseman Miguel Pena leading the charge, the Denver Rocketeers got a road win over the Chicago Fire, 9-8, in Game 1 of the Grand Championship Series today. Pena was 1-3 with a home run and 2 walks. He scored 3 runs and drove in 2 at Chicago Grounds to help Denver take a 1-0 lead in the best-of-7 series.

Denver needed the clutch hitting of Brent Arena to notch the win. The first baseman hammered a grand slam home run in the top of the seventh inning to put the Rocketeers on top, 7-6. For the game, Arena had 1 home run and 1 single in 4 at-bats.

"We swung the bats good throughout the lineup," said Denver skipper Rafael Loza.

The next game is set to be played tomorrow at Chicago Grounds in Chicago.
