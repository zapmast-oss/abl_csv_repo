Definitions:
  smallball_index = 0.6*steal_plus + 0.4*bunt_plus (plus metrics use league avg as 1.00).
  sb_attempts_pg = (SB + CS) per estimated game (starter count fallback).
  sh_pg = sac bunts per estimated game.
  quick_hook_rate = share of starts <5 IP with <=3 ER; hook index compares to league rate.
  avg_ip_start = innings per start (context for hook decisions).
  platoon_usage_rate = |PA vs RHP - PA vs LHP| / total PA (lineup-derived when splits absent).
  manager_index = average of the three indices above (available components only).
