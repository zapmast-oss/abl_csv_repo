# ABL 1981 – “It’s Monday” Video Structure

This document defines the **standard weekly video rhythm** and how **special weeks** (like the Week 6 relaunch with extra featured games) extend that pattern.

---

## 1. Normal Week – Standard 5-Video Rhythm

For a normal week, the Action Baseball League “It’s Monday” package is **five videos**:

### Video 1 – It’s Monday (Week N)

- Previous week’s results & highlights (via website/scoreboard).
- OOTP Home Screen:
  - Power Rankings Top 9 (read directly from the League News panel).
  - ABC Player of the Week article.
  - NBC Player of the Week article.
- Home Screen team stat leaders (by conference, selected stats, 1–2 format).
- Standings Screen:
  - Player stat leaders (by conference, 1–2 format).
  - Division standings (1st place and 2nd place with games back).

This video “sets the table” for the whole week.

### Video 2 – Sim Thru Saturday

- From the Scores screen:
  - Sim the games from Monday through Saturday for the current week.
  - Provide light commentary on key outcomes (streaks, sweeps, upsets, etc.).
- By the end of this video, **all games through Saturday are complete**.

### Video 3 – Showcase Sunday Game 1

- Full, called broadcast of the **first Sunday featured matchup**.
- Focus on storylines and key players from the two clubs.

### Video 4 – Showcase Sunday Game 2

- Full, called broadcast of the **second Sunday featured matchup**.
- Same treatment as Game 1: attention to context, standings, and individual performances.

### Video 5 – Real-Time Sim + Stats To Go

- Real-time sim of the **remaining Sunday games**.
- “Stats To Go” wrap:
  - Key league-wide notes after the week’s final games.
  - Brief look at how Sunday’s results affected races and leaders.

At this point, **the week is complete**, and the next major step is the SupSim / WeekMiner work for subsequent weeks.

---

## 2. Special Relaunch Week – Week 6 (Extra Featured Games)

Week 6 is a **special relaunch week** with **additional featured games on Monday and Tuesday**, which means **more videos** than a normal 5-video week.

The **core roles** remain the same:

1. It’s Monday – Week 6 overview.  
2. Sim Thru Saturday (conceptually).  
3. Showcase Sunday Game 1.  
4. Showcase Sunday Game 2.  
5. Real-Time Sim + Stats To Go.

But around those anchors, **extra videos are inserted** for the Monday and Tuesday featured games.

### Week 6 Target Layout – 10 Videos

**Video 1 – It’s Monday: Week 6**

- Same structure as the normal **Video 1**:
  - Previous week’s results & highlights (website).
  - Home Screen:
    - Power Rankings Top 9.
    - ABC Player of the Week.
    - NBC Player of the Week.
    - Team stat leaders by conference (selected stats, 1–2 format).
  - Standings Screen:
    - Player stat leaders by conference (1–2 format).
    - Division standings (1st & 2nd with games back).

---

### Monday – Two Featured Games + Monday Sim Wrap

**Video 2 – Monday Featured Game 1**

- Full called broadcast of the first Monday featured matchup.

**Video 3 – Monday Featured Game 2**

- Full called broadcast of the second Monday featured matchup.

**Video 4 – Monday Sim Wrap**

- Sim the **rest of Monday’s games**.
- Quick rundown of notable results.

---

### Tuesday – Two Featured Games + Sim Through Saturday

**Video 5 – Tuesday Featured Game 1**

- Full called broadcast of the first Tuesday featured matchup.

**Video 6 – Tuesday Featured Game 2**

- Full called broadcast of the second Tuesday featured matchup.

**Video 7 – Tuesday Sim Wrap (Through Saturday)**

- Sim:
  - The remaining Tuesday games.
  - Then Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, and Saturday.
- Light commentary on big swings and key outcomes.
- By the end, all games through **Saturday of Week 6** are complete.

---

### Sunday – Showcase + Real-Time Sim

**Video 8 – Showcase Sunday Game 1**

- Full called broadcast of the first Showcase Sunday game.

**Video 9 – Showcase Sunday Game 2**

- Full called broadcast of the second Showcase Sunday game.

**Video 10 – Sunday Real-Time Sim + Stats To Go**

- Real-time sim of the remaining Sunday games.
- “Stats To Go” closing segment:
  - Final summary of Week 6.
  - High-level takeaway heading into the SupSim / WeekMiner work for Weeks 7 and 8.

---

## 3. Summary

- **Normal weeks** use a **5-video structure**:
  - 1: It’s Monday
  - 2: Sim Thru Saturday
  - 3–4: Sunday showcase games
  - 5: Real-Time Sim + Stats To Go

- **Special relaunch weeks** (like Week 6) **add extra featured-game videos** on Monday and Tuesday, expanding the package (e.g., to 10 videos) while keeping the same core anchors:
  - It’s Monday overview
  - Full week sim completed before Sunday
  - Two Sunday showcases
  - Real-time sim finish with Stats To Go
